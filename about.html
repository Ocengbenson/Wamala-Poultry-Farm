About us

Background


Wamala Poultry was founded in 2010 as a poultry breeder farm and has grown steadily over the years to establish itself as one of the best- known brands in the Northern Ugandan poultry industry.
We are now the leading supplier of day-old chicks, Chickens, Eggs and poultry feeds in the country.

We have recently started producing other livestock feeds such as dairy and pig feeds. A commercial broiler farm was set up in 2016 which is now in full production.
